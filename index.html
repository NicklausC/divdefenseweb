Counter-UAS Missile Use

Missiles are a costly defense system, especially so when used against small unmanned hobbyist drones that can cost less than $100. While effictive, missiles are impractical to use as a C-UAS system. Using a missle to kill a drone can cost millions.

Counter-UAS Laser Use

Lasers as a C-UAS weapon are rapidly developing and becoming better by the day. Lasers also occupy a fraction of the space as missile batteries. Using a laser to kill a drone can cost pennies. These factors make lasers a superior solution.
