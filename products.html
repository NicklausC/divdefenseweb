Gladius

The C-UAS Gladius system is an autonomous counter unmanned aerial system that’s priority is providing over watch for warfighters using precision guided energy weapons. The design of our product is a large hex copter drone with a gimbal armed with high-wattage lasers and a directional emp. The drone arms are structured of woven carbon fiber and covered at irregular angles with Kevlar to protect from small arms and shrapnel while also being coated in radar evading paint. Components known to have high heat; battery cells, electronic speed controllers, video transmitters, and brushless motors, are covered with aerogel fiber to lower heat signature and improve running performance. The drone’s individual propellers are carbon fiber to increase strength and shaped as to decrease noise in in the best possible way. The drone is powered by lithium polymer graphene batteries to provide optimal flight time and power throughout use. The end package is a durable autonomous system with built in safeties meant to either defend a geographic area or defend warfighters from guerilla attacks caused by small UAVs while also being silent and as if it were not there in the first place.
